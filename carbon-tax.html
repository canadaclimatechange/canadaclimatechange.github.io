Carbon Tax

Carbon Tax is a tax that the Canadian government has put into place prevent gas emissions from entering the atmosphere.

Canada is using carbon tax as a tactic to reduce climate change. Money talks, so putting a tax of fossil fuel emissions can lead to changes such as, people using public transit or buying energy efficient products.

- It will lower green-house gas emissions

- Individuals may start using public transportation

- It encourages the use of other cleaner energy sources such as flow resources
