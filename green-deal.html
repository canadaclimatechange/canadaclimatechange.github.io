Green New Deal

- The Green New Deal is a deal that wants to acknowledge the fact that we are facing major environmental issues

- Canada wants to step up to this issue to try and influence other countries to follow

- Paris and Canada wants to target  reducing gas emissions by at least 50%  by 2030

- The biggest challenge that will be faced is accepting the fact as humans that oil and gas are not unlimited

- Trying to get rid of oil and gas from the Canadian economy

- Make this a topic that political leaders discuss in meetings

- Taking Climate Change seriously

- Showing that even though Canada is a resource based economy we can still make major change

- We need to set a serious example for the rest of the world as Canada is one of the biggest role models of the world
