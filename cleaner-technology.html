Canada and Cleaner Technology

What Will it Enhance?

Factual Projections

Cleaner technology is an industry that is starting to grow significantly and Canada is one of the big leaders in this competition. Canada is a resource based country and many countries world wide depend on their resources such as minerals and fuels for they're own use.  If they set an impression on the theory of cleaner technology it might inspire others to make the same change conquering climate change as one.

Anyone would think that spending on cleaner technology is a waste of time and money, actually it enhances many things as a whole. It will cause growth in economy. For Example, it will create more job opportunities for the future generations. It will also provide environmental protection. The cleaner technology will reduce the amount of greenhouse gases that are being put into the atmosphere.

- "Clean energy is already one of the fastest growing sectors of our economy. Canada’s clean energy sector has 20 times as much wind energy capacity and 125 times as much solar electricity capacity as it did a decade ago"

 

- "The Canadian clean technology market generated about $11.6 billion in revenue in 2014, and exports accounted for half of these revenues. The 2016 Canadian Clean Technology Industry Report estimates that the clean energy sector directly employs 55,600 Canadians and analysts have suggested that, with support, the number of jobs in the sector could reach 76,000 in 2022."

 

- "This fall, the Government of Canada announced $21.9 billion over 11 years for green infrastructure which will include targeted investments to support greenhouse gas reductions and enable greater climate change adaptation and increased resilience.

Canada’s prosperity depends on a healthy environment. Strategic investments today will spur economic growth in the short and long term, while protecting the environment and well-being of Canadians."
